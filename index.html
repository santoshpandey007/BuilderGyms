About Us
Builder Gyms Pvt. Ltd. is a chain of gyms spreaded around in the city of Bangalore.
We started our first gym in 1999 in the heart of Bengaluru at Hebbal. Since then there was no looking back. We have one of the largest chain of Gymnasiums in the world. The number of gymnasiums we own in Bengaluru is well above 65. The average number of customers we can accomodate at any of our gymnasium goes upto 232. Some of our gyms are built at greater heights in order to decrease the oxygen levels inside the gym, so the people can get the best out of their body and burn the fat faster.
